# Universal Lending Policy v1.0

### 1. Identity Verification
- Applicant must be "VERIFIED" via the identity tool.
- If verification fails, the loan must be REJECTED.

### 2. Credit Score Requirements
- Minimum acceptable credit score is 640.
- Scores between 640 and 700 require a manual comment regarding risk.

### 3. Loan-to-Value (LTV) Limits
- Maximum LTV is 90%.
- Formula: (Loan Amount / Property Value) * 100.

### 4. Debt-to-Income (DTI) Ratios
- Monthly gross income must be at least 3x the estimated monthly loan payment.
- Estimated monthly payment is calculated as 10% of the loan amount (simplified).
