# Generative AI vs Agentic AI

## Video
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EDb37y_MhRw

## Summarize
| Type | Description & Example |
|-----|-----|
| **Generative AI** | **Description:** Generative AI is a tool designed to create content (text, images, or code) based on a prompt. <br><br> **Example:** You ask for a 3-day itinerary for Tokyo. It instantly generates a detailed list of shrines, sushi spots, and museums. However, it cannot actually book anything for you; it just provides the information you requested. |
| **Agentic AI** | **Description:** Agentic AI is a digital worker designed to achieve a goal by planning and executing multiple steps autonomously. <br><br> **Example:** You give it a goal: “Book me a 3-day trip to Tokyo within a $2,000 budget.” The AI searches live flight prices, compares hotel availability, checks your calendar for conflicts, and executes the bookings through various APIs. |
